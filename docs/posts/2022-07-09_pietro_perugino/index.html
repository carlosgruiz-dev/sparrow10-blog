Pietro Perugino (Quote)

This is a quote from chapter 41 of the book “The 48 Laws of Power” in a text from Giorgio Vasari talking about Pietro Perugino.

… that after bad weather, good weather must follow, and that during the good weather houses must be built for shelter in times of need.
